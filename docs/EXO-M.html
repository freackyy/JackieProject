Meet EXO-M

EXO-M (M stands for Mandarin) is the Chinese subgroup of a Chinese-South Korean boy band EXO produced by S.M. Entertainment. EXO-M promoted their music in China and consisted of four Chinese namely Kris, Tao, Luhan, and Lay and two Korean members namely Xiumin and Chen. The group is currently inactive due to three of its six members which are Kris, Tao, and Luhan departing from EXO.
