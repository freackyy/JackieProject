Meet EXO - SC

EXO-SC (Sehun and Chanyeol stylized as EXO-SC) is the second official subgroup of South Korean-Chinese boy band EXO. Formed by SM Entertainment in 2019, the group composed of two members Chanyeol and Sehun. Their debut extended play What a Life was released on July 22, 2019.

Chanyeol (찬열)

Main Vocalist, Main Rapper, Visual

Sehun (세훈)

Main Dancer, Lead Rapper, Maknae, Visual
