Meet EXO - K

EXO-K (K stands for Korean) is the Korean subgroup of the Chinese-South Korean boy band EXO produced by S.M. Entertainment. EXO-K promoted thei music in Korea and consists of six Korean members namely Suho, D.O, Chanyeol, Sehun, Kai, and Baekhyun. The subgroup is currently inactive and now only promotes as a group due to the three members of EXO-M leaving EXO.
