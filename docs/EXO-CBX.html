Meet EXO-CBX

EXO-CBX (Exo-Chen-Baek-Xi; stylized as EXO-CBX) is the first official sub-unit of South Korean-Chinese boy group EXO. Formed by SM Entertainment in 2016, the group is composed of three members: Chen, Baekhyun, and Xiumin. Their debut extended play Hey Mama! was released on October 31, 2016.
